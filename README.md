📌 X-Culture Global Research Project

📖 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the X-Culture Global Collaboration Program (Sep 2025 – Oct 2025), focusing on international business analysis skills and cross-cultural teamwork.

🎯 Objective

To analyse global market trends using secondary research and develop structured business recommendations through international team collaboration.

🛠️ Methods & Tools

Secondary research (industry journals, analyst reports, regulatory publications)
Data organisation and synthesis
Collaborative tools for task coordination and communication

👥 Team Collaboration

Worked with team members across multiple countries and time zones
Used shared trackers and regular updates to manage tasks
Coordinated deliverables through structured communication and planning

📊 Key Contributions

Conducted research and extracted insights from multiple business and industry sources
Organised findings into structured recommendations for final reporting
Ensured timely completion of deliverables through effective coordination
